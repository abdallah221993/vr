🚀 HOW TO USE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Open: https://3000-idw7uj8wjqy46tvbokh48-5c13a017.sandbox.novita.ai

2. Select furniture items from the grid

3. Click "تفعيل الواقع المعزز" (Activate AR)

4. Grant camera permission

5. Point camera at Hiro marker

6. Enjoy! All selected items will appear together
